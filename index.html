Mechanophysiology Lab

Research Group of Dr. John Georgiadis, R. A. Pritzker Professor of Biomedical Engineering

Illinois Tech, Chicago USA

We specialize in Biomedical Imaging and Biomechanics, and use advanced experimental and computational methods to connect tissue microstructure and mechanical properties to its physiology and function.

Representative Publications

  • Agarwal, et al. (2025). Data-Driven Depiction of Aging Related Physiological Volume Shrinkage in Brain White Matter.
  • Naughton, et al. (2024). Accelerated, physics-inspired inference of skeletal muscle microstructure from diffusion-weighted MRI.
  • Naughton, et al. (2020). Lattice Boltzmann method for simulation of diffusion magnetic resonance imaging physics in multiphase tissue models.
